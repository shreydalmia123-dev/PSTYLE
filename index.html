About i.

A quiet hand between collectors and India’s rising studios.

Pankhuri Dalmia — art consultant, Mumbai. Working with painters in Bombay, Baroda, Delhi, and Kolkata.

Pankhuri at the studio, Bandra · 2025

I’ve always believed that art isn’t décor — it’s emotion, memory, and presence in a room you live in every day.

PSTYLE is my practice. I work directly with upcoming Indian artists to bring original, considered paintings into homes, offices, and private collections. Every piece in the catalog is available to commission or acquire — and I personally handle the conversation with the artist, the medium and sizing, framing, and delivery.

If a painting catches your eye, send a note. I’ll share more on who made it, what it’s painted on, what it costs, and how to make it yours. Collecting should feel personal, not transactional.

Collecting ii.

How acquiring a piece works.

No galleries, no pressure. Just a conversation, a clear price, and a painting on your wall.

01

Tell me what you’re drawn to.

Send the title of a piece from the catalog — or the room, light, and feeling you’d like to live with. I’ll come back with options.

02

I’ll bring you the artist.

I share the artist’s story, medium and provenance, true sizes, and a fair price. Custom sizes and commissions are welcomed.

03

Framed, packed, on your wall.

Conservation framing, white-glove packing, and delivery anywhere in India — international on request. You only pick the spot.

What to include in your note
